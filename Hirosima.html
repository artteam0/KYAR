Actors and participants

Natasha Gorbas

Mother of three children

Info

Born in Moscow. Graduated from the Moscow Art Theater School-Studio, acting department, course of Igor Zolotovitsky. Actress of the Ermolova Theater. She taught speech and acting, preparing graduates for admission to theater institutes. She collaborated with the Meyerhold Center, International Memorial, and the Open Stage project. Participated in the festival “Lyubimovka”, “Season of Stanislavsky”. Participant of the Summer Theater School of the Russian Theater Union. Regular participant in the play “Brodsky Poetry” (PTT Theatre). As a director, she produced the play “The Blind” based on the play by Maurice Maeterlinck, which took place in the Boyar Chambers of the Union of Artists of the Russian Federation. Actress of the independent theater project “Daughters of SOSO”.

Natasha Gorbas

Marietta Tsigal-Pol.

A priest who runs around

Info

Born on October 14, 1984 in Moscow. In 2006 she graduated from RATI, course of S.A. Golomazov. She played at the Nikitsky Gate Theater and the Modern Theater. Since 2007 in the troupe of the Theater on Malaya Bronnaya. Theater named after Mayakovsky: Ms. Pernel - "Tartuffe" Theater "Modern": Nastasya Petrovna - "Uncle's Dream". Theater at Nikitsky Gate: Former dachshund - "Dogs" Wife - "Police" Young Jewish Woman - "Gambrinus" Rene - "Drinking Alone"

Marietta Tsigal-Pol.

Elena Makhova

Another priest

Info

Born on December 18, 1985 in Novokuznetsk. After graduating from school, she studied at the acting department of EGTI for 2 years, and in 2010 she graduated from the directing department of RATI-GITIS (acting group of O. Kudryashov’s workshop). Serves in the Praktika theater and the Bulgakov House theater.

Skirina Yulia

Info
